Rules of openNews

openNews is a free sharing website. We have some rules set in place to prevent this from being abused.

General People:

  • 1) Never spam. Ever.
  • 2) Do not "doxx" people, or release any personal info.
  • 3) Do not participate in brigading, witch hunting, or anything of the like.
  • 4) Do not use copyrighted materials
  • 5) Do share links with your friends. Spread the word!
  • 6) Currently, we do not have a way to automatically post things, so all posts are approved manually. Think you can help us with that? Go Here.

Governments:

  • 1) This website is using the first amendment and is USA based, do not attempt to take anything down.
  • 2) Don't spread misinformation
