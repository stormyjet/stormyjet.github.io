new experimental thought analyisis

ThoughtGrab is a new technology that can grab thoughts from a mind. It grabs these and can be utilized in many things.

How to start:


ThoughtGrab can be initialzied using thought(grab); or the new shorthand think(on);

How to use:


TG can be used by simple things, and this is sample code: thought(grab);
thought(analyze);
get(results); create( results.tg.js( using(stdHTML))); stop
